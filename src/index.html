Hendrik Heerschap

Zevendalseweg 12 · Plasmolen, 6586AS · The Netherlands

My adventure in the aviation industry started in Greece with Global aviation. Here I did my integrated ATPL. Unfortunately, I finished my flight training during the Covid pandemic and the airline jobs were not abundantly available, so I decided to continue my training to become a flight instructor. This training I completed in Budapest, Hungary, and I was lucky to find a flight instructor job quickly after finishing my training. The job was with a start-up company/flight school E-Flight Academy in my home country, the Netherlands, and I gained a lot of experience here flying electric aircrafts. Eventually the aviation industry slowly started to recover and decided to chase my dream of flying commercially and passed my interview with Wizz Air where I started as a First Officer based in Gdańsk, Poland.
